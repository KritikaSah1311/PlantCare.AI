About Plant Disease Detection System

Our advanced AI model analyzes plant leaf images to detect diseases and health conditions with 95% accuracy. Early detection enables farmers to take preventive measures, reduce crop loss, and improve yield. The system is trained on thousands of plant disease images from agricultural research institutions and can identify over 3000 different plant diseases and conditions.
